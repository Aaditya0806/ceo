Our Expertise in Bulk Hiring

  • Comprehensive Talent Pool: Access a diverse database of pre-screened candidates across industries and roles.

  • Customized Hiring Processes: Tailored strategies designed to meet specific requirements, timelines, and scales.

  • End-to-End Management: From sourcing to onboarding, we manage every aspect of the hiring process for a seamless experience.

  • Cutting-Edge Assessment Tools: Advanced evaluation techniques, including psychometric tests, neuromuscular tests, aptitude tests, and group exercises, to identify best-fit candidates.

Proven Track Record

  • 45,000+ Placements: Successfully completed across industries.

  • Industry Expertise: Specialized in manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, FMCG, and IT sectors.

Case Studies

OLA Electric

  • Challenge: Navigate the post-COVID hiring landscape to scale operations for their E-Scooter manufacturing facility.

  • Solution: Assessed nearly 10,000 female candidates across Tamil Nadu, onboarding 1,950 talented individuals.

  • Impact: Enabled seamless commissioning of the facility with a workforce ready to deliver from day one.

Pfizer India

  • Challenge: Staff a greenfield plant in Vizag with qualified female candidates within a 150 km range.

  • Solution: Assessed over 2,500 candidates with Intermediate Science qualifications.

  • Outcome: Successfully onboarded 250 women within three months, meeting workforce requirements effectively.

Coke India

  • Challenge: Hire skilled personnel for three greenfield plants across India (Sanand, Siliguri, Hyderabad).

  • Solution: Evaluated over 2,500 candidates with Chemical and Food Technology Diplomas.

  • Outcome: Delivered 250 hires within three months, ensuring timely operational readiness.

Our Bulk Hiring Process

  • Requirement Analysis: Understand workforce needs, role-specific requirements, and timelines.

  • Talent Sourcing: Identify candidates through extensive networks, partnerships, and databases.

  • Assessment & Screening: Utilize customized evaluations, including cognitive, technical, and physical readiness tests.

  • Selection & Onboarding: Manage documentation, orientation, and integration for a hassle-free onboarding experience.
