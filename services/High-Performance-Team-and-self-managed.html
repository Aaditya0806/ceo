Empowering Organizations Through High-Performance Teams

At CEO HR Consultancy , we specialize in transforming organizations by building High-Performance Teams (HPT) and Self-Managed Teams (SMT). These pioneering approaches optimize the synergy between people, work, technology, and information. Our proven methodology fosters knowledge, commitment, and flexibility, delivering outstanding results across industries.

Why Choose HPT/SMT?

Our temp-to-hire programs offer the flexibility to assess candidates on the job before making a permanent hiring decision, minimizing risk & ensuring a cultural fit.

Grassroots Ownership

Empowered teams take ownership, enhancing safety, quality, and profitability.

Flattened Hierarchies

Streamlined decision-making improves responsiveness and agility.

Seamless Integration

Break down silos, reduce turnaround times, and drive cross-functional collaboration.

Proactive Relationships

Zero industrial unrest achieved in client organizations over a decade.

Best Practices Hub

Accelerate adoption of Lean, Six Sigma, and World-Class Manufacturing (WCM).

Core Principles of HPT/SMT

High Involvement Principles

Egalitarianism, shared information, performance-reward linkage, and knowledge development.

Employee Empowerment

Teams set goals aligned with the organization’s mission, replacing traditional supervision.

Sociotechnical Foundation

Rooted in principles established by the Tavistock Institute, UK.
