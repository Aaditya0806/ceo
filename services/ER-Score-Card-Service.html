Introduction to ER Score Card

The ER Score Card is a comprehensive system designed to proactively manage Industrial Relations (IR) in Indian factories. By auditing employee relations with advanced tools, real-time data synchronization, and actionable reporting, it fosters a harmonious and productive work environment.
With Android-based audits and cloud synchronization, the ER Score Card provides immediate insights, detailed dashboards, and actionable alerts, enabling management to address concerns promptly and maintain compliance.
