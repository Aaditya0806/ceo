Privacy Policy

At Centre for Excellence in Organization Private Limited, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, phone number, and details provided through contact forms.
  • Usage Data: Data about how you interact with our website, collected through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and enhance our services.
  • To respond to inquiries and requests.
  • To send updates, promotional content, and information aligned with your interests.

3. Data Sharing and Security

  • Data Protection:
    • No sale or rental of your data to third parties.
    • Data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.
  • Trusted Partners:
    • Information may be shared with trusted partners under strict confidentiality agreements for service delivery.
  • Legal Disclaimer on Data Sharing:
    • Data sharing complies with legal requirements, including those related to lawful requests by authorities.
    • Users are notified when their data is shared, except where legally prohibited.

4. Handling of User Data

Data Collection
  • Personal data is collected via forms you fill out on our website.
  • Usage data is collected through cookies and analytics.
Data Usage
  • Providing Services: Data is used to deliver services and enhance user experience.
  • Communication: Enables updates, promotional content, and tailored information.
Data Security Measures
  • Encryption ensures data protection in transit and at rest.
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular audits, data anonymization, and intrusion detection safeguard against risks.
  • Compliance with GDPR, IT Act, and secure software practices reinforces legal adherence.

5. Legal Basis for Data Processing

  • Consent: Data is processed based on explicit user consent where required.
  • Legitimate Interest: Data is processed for security, service delivery, and user experience improvements.

6. User Rights

  • Right to access, correct, or delete personal data.
  • Option to opt-out of promotional communications.

7. Cookies and Tracking

  • Cookies store preferences, enhance functionality, and deliver personalized content.
  • Users can manage or disable cookies via browser settings, though some features may be affected.
  • Third-party cookies are used for analytics and advertising to provide relevant content.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Please revisit this page to stay informed.
